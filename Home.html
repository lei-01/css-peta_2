HANAN

The Morning Goddess

Hanan is a revered goddess in Philippine mythology, particularly in Visayan beliefs, known as the gentle goddess of dawn, morning, and new beginnings. She is believed to guide the first light of day and prepare the path for the rising sun, bringing hope and clarity to the world. Often associated with guidance and safe journeys, Hanan represents quiet strength and renewal. Her story highlights themes of optimism, balance, and resilience, symbolizing the transition from darkness to light and the promise that every new day offers a fresh beginning.
