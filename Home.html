The celestial goddess who stands between darkness and daylight

Hope Rises with the Dawn

In the depths of Philippine seas and rivers lives a legendary being feared and admired for generations—the Sirena. Before the sun rises, before light touches the land, a gentle goddess awakens the world. Step into the legend of Hanan, the keeper of dawn, the bringer of light, and the guardian of hope. Each morning she walks silently across the sky, spreading warmth, guiding travelers, and reminding all beings that every day is a chance to start anew.
